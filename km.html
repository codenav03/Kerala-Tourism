KOLLAM

Kollam, also known as Quilon, is a historic port city located in the southern part of Kerala, India. Situated along the Malabar Coast, Kollam is renowned for its scenic backwaters, serene beaches, and rich cultural heritage..

Culturally, Kollam is renowned for its vibrant festivals and art forms. The annual Kollam Pooram, held at the Asramam Maidan, is a grand celebration featuring colorful processions, traditional music, and mesmerizing fireworks. The city is also a hub for traditional performing arts such as Kathakali, Mohiniyattam, and Koodiyattam, with several cultural centers and academies dedicated to preserving these ancient art forms. With its blend of natural beauty, historical significance, and cultural vibrancy, Kollam offers visitors a unique and unforgettable experience that captures the essence of Kerala's rich heritage.
