THRISSUR

Thrissur, often referred to as the "Cultural Capital of Kerala," is a vibrant city in the heart of the state. Situated in the central part of Kerala, Thrissur is renowned for its rich cultural heritage, religious diversity, and traditional festivals.

Thrissur's bustling markets, such as the famous Thrissur Round and the Chalai Market, offer a vibrant shopping experience with a variety of traditional handicrafts, spices, and textiles. With its blend of tradition and modernity, Thrissur continues to be a cultural hub and a must-visit destination for those seeking to explore the rich heritage of Kerala.
