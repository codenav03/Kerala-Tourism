KOTTAYAM

Kottayam, nestled in the heart of Kerala, India, is a district renowned for its scenic beauty, rich cultural heritage, and educational institutions. Often referred to as the "Land of Letters, Latex, and Lakes," Kottayam offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity.

In addition to its cultural and educational significance, Kottayam is renowned for its delicious cuisine, with mouthwatering dishes like appam, stew, and fish curry being popular among locals and tourists alike. The district is also famous for its traditional toddy shops, where visitors can savor authentic Kerala cuisine and locally brewed toddy. With its tranquil backwaters, religious landmarks, literary legacy, and culinary delights, Kottayam offers visitors a diverse and enriching experience that captures the essence of Kerala's vibrant culture and heritage.
